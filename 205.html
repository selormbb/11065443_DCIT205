Welcome To The University Of Ghana Computer Science Department

We offer long time and short time courses for people to partake in. Some branches of the courses are listed

  • Short Courses
  • Undergraduate Courses
  • MPhil and Msc Programs
  • PhD Programs

Short courses

Condensed educational programs that concentrate on particular topics within the field of computer science are known as short courses. These courses, which usually last a few weeks to a few months, provide focused education for people who want to grow better at a certain field of computer science without committing to a complete degree program. We cover a wide range of topics, including web development, data analysis, cybersecurity, machine learning, and programming languages like Python and Java. We give certificates to demonstrate newly gained skills. We also frequently offer practical exercises and opportunity for hands-on learning.

Undergraduate Courses

Typically the University Of Ghana offers this course for a period of 4 years leading to a Bachelor of Science (BSc) or Bachelor of Arts (BA) degree in Computer Science.

This course usually covers some fields like
  • Programming Fundamentals
  • Digital And Logical Systems Design
  • Database Management
  • Multi Media And Web Design

MPhil and Msc Programs

MPhil (Master of Philosophy) and MSc (Master of Science) programs in computer science are postgraduate degrees that offer deeper specialization and research opportunities compared to undergraduate studies.

PhD Programs

PhD programs in the University Of Ghana Computer science are the highest level of academic study one can undertake in the field. They are research-intensive and typically take around 4 years to complete.

Some Courses Include;
  • Research Focus
  • Research Propsal
  • Publication And Presentation
