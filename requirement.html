ENTRY REQUIREMENT

To be able to qualify for undergraduate courses these are the requirements The course is in two types
  • Bsc Information Technology
    • students must get credit passes in English,Mathematics,Integrated Science and Social.They must have credit passes in Elective Mathematics and any two elective courses
  • BSc Mathematical Sciences
    • students must get credit passes in English,Mathematics,Integrated Science and Social.They must have credit passes in Elective Mathematics and any two elective courses To be able to get Computer Science in Level 200 you must have passed the BSc Mathematical Science courses and have a CGPA above 1.00
